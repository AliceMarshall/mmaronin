mma /mixed martial arts/ n. mixed martial arts encompasses stand up and striking: boxing, kickboxing and Muay Thai, and grappling and ground work: Brazilian Jiu Jitsu and wrestling, combined with the respect and discipline from traditional martial arts such as karate, taekwondo and judo.

ronin / n. a wandering samurai with no master.

Adult Classes

Team Ronin offers high level mixed martial arts training for complete beginners as well as seasoned professionals. In our weekly group adult classes, we cover the technical fundamentals and drills for stand up and striking as well as ground work and grappling. All our sessions end with free style sparring.

While all sports carry an element of risk, Team Ronin aims to insure the safety of all our members. Sparring should always be controlled and non-contact sparring is also available.
